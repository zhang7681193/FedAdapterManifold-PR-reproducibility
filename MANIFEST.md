## Archive structure

The archive contains:

- `configs/`: YAML experiment configurations for audited settings.
- `fedadaptermanifold/`: reusable implementation modules.
- `scripts/`: experiment, aggregation, plotting, audit, and packaging scripts.
- `tests/`: focused tests for reproducibility-critical utilities.
- `results/`: audited CSV summaries and compact result artifacts.
- `figures/` and `paper_figures/`: generated figure assets used in the manuscript.
- `reports/`: experiment fairness/stability audit notes.
- manuscript source snapshots and references for traceability.

## Reproducibility notes

1. The package is intended to reproduce the reported diagnostics, tables, figures, and compact audit summaries.
2. Full reruns require local access to the relevant benchmark datasets and their licenses.
3. The package does not include raw PACS, OfficeHome, DomainNet, or MedMNIST images.
4. The manuscript reports audited five-seed summaries and paired client-seed statistics; compact result CSVs are included where possible.
